About Us

Complimate Advisory Services is a Practicing Company Secretaries Firm located in Pune. We provide quality services for solving various Corporate Law and Secretarial matters. We also endeavor to widen and maintain our client base by adhering to the highest professional standards. The people associated with the firm strive to cross new horizons by exploiting new avenues of services through new learning. For this purpose, they keep themselves abreast with the frequent changes in law.

Vision

To be a professional services firm, providing cost effective legal and business solutions through a community of learned and qualified individuals.

Mission

  • Excellence: We define excellence as “being the best at what we do” by imbibing the spirit of constant improvement in all our endeavors to achieve high quality results and set standards.
  • Professionalism: As a professional organization, we develop and sustain the competence of our people. We are objective and fair in our approach, consistent in our performance and own responsibility for our actions.
  • Ethics: We act with utmost integrity and believe in honesty and respect for one and all. We believe it as our responsibility to be transparent and straight forward in our dealings.
  • Transparency: We believe in providing a service with transparency and believe in fast and in time communication with the clients.
